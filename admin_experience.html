How to export your LinkedIn data:

  1. Go to LinkedIn → Settings & Privacy → Data Privacy → Get a copy of your data
  2. Select "Want something in particular? Select the data files you want"
  3. Check "Positions" or "Profile"
  4. Click "Request archive"
  5. LinkedIn will email you a download link (usually within 10 minutes)
  6. Extract the ZIP and upload the "Positions.csv" or the JSON files
